''''''''''Employees''''''''''
' Employee has multiple priviledges 
Employees "0..*" -- "0..*" Privileges : "has"

' Employee holds 1 title 
Employees "0..*" --> "1" Titles : "Holds title"

' Self-association: An Employee reports to 1 Supervisor; a Supervisor may have >0 subordinates
Employees "0..*" --> "1" Employees : "Reports to"

' An Order is processed by one Employee
Orders "0..*" --> "1" Employees : "Processed by"

' A PurchaseOrder is submitted by one Employee
PurchaseOrders "0..*" --> "1" Employees : "Submitted by" 

' A PurchaseOrder is approved by one Employee
PurchaseOrders "0..*" --> "1" Employees : "Approved by" 


''''''''''Orders''''''''''
' An Order uses one TaxStatus; a TaxStatus is used by 0..* Orders
Orders "0..*" --> "1" TaxStatuses : "Uses tax status"

' An Order is shipped by one Company (shipper); a Company ships 0..* Orders
Orders "0..*" --> "1" Shippers : "Shipped by"

' An Order has one OrderStatus; an OrderStatus applies to 0..* Orders
Orders "0..*" --> "1" OrderStatuses : "Has status"

'An Order is composed of 1..* OrderDetails; OrderDetail belongs exclusively to one Order
Orders "1" *-- "1..*" OrderDetails : "Part of"

' An Order is placed by one Company (Customer)
Orders "0..*" --> "1" Customers : "Placed by" 


''''''''''Companies''''''''''
' A Company is classified as one CompanyType; a CompanyType classifies 0..* Companies
Companies "0..*" --> "1" CompanyTypes : "Classified as"

' A Company is located in one State; a State has 0..* Companies
Companies "0..*" --> "1" States : "Located in"

' A Company uses one TaxStatus; a TaxStatus is used by 0..* Companies
Companies "0..*" --> "1" TaxStatuses : "Uses tax status"

' A PurchaseOrder is sent to one Company (Vendor)
PurchaseOrders "0..*" --> "1" Vendors : "Ordered from"

' A Contact is associated with one Company; a Company can have 0..* Contacts
Contacts "0..*" --> "1" Companies : "Associated with"

'Vendors may supply multiple products; multiple products may have the same vendor
Products "0..*" -- "0..*" Vendors : < supplies


''''''''''Products''''''''''
' Products belongs to one ProductCategory; a category has 0..* Products
Products "0..*" --> "1" ProductCategories : "Belongs to"

' Each StockTake record records inventory for one Product; a Product can have 0..* StockTake records
StockTakes "0..*" --> "1" Products : "Records inventory of"

' An OrderDetail includes one Product; a Product can appear in 0..* OrderDetails
OrderDetails "0..*" --> "1" Products : "Includes"

' A PurchaseOrderDetail contains one Product; a Product can appear in 0..* PurchaseOrderDetails
PurchaseOrderDetails "0..*" --> "1" Products : "Contains"


''''''''''Purchase Orders''''''''''
' A PurchaseOrder has one PurchaseOrderStatus; a PurchaseOrderStatus applies to 0..* PurchaseOrders
PurchaseOrders "0..*" --> "1" PurchaseOrderStatuses : "Has status"

'A PurchaseOrder is composed of 1..* PurchaseOrderDetails; details belong exclusively to one PurchaseOrder
PurchaseOrders "1" *-- "1..*" PurchaseOrderDetails : "Details of"


''''''''''OrderDetails''''''''''
' An OrderDetail has one OrderDetailStatus; an OrderDetailStatus is used by 0..* OrderDetails
OrderDetails "0..*" --> "1" OrderDetailStatuses : "Has status"
